Morphological characteristics contain sufficient discriminatory information to accurately classify dry bean varieties. A custom Multi-Layer Perceptron Neural Network with architecture 16–64–32–16–7 achieved approximately 90.18% average cross-validation accuracy on the Dry Bean Dataset. The model successfully learned morphological patterns of seven bean varieties and demonstrated strong classification performance, highlighting the effectiveness of neural networks for agricultural automation, crop quality assessment, and intelligent bean classification systems.
